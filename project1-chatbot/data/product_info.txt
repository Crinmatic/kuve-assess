PRODUCT INFORMATION - TechSoft Pro Suite

OVERVIEW
TechSoft Pro Suite is a comprehensive project management and collaboration platform designed for modern teams. 
Our software helps you manage projects, track tasks, collaborate with team members, and analyze productivity metrics.

VERSION HISTORY
- Current Version: 5.2.1 (Released October 2024)
- Previous Version: 5.1.0 (Released July 2024)
- Major Features in 5.2: Enhanced mobile experience, AI-powered task suggestions, improved reporting dashboard

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
Operating Systems:
- Windows 10 or later
- macOS 11 (Big Sur) or later
- Linux (Ubuntu 20.04+, Fedora 34+)

Browser Requirements (Web Version):
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

Hardware:
- Minimum 4GB RAM (8GB recommended)
- 500MB free disk space
- Internet connection required for sync
